一剪梅
Yì jiǎn méi 
A Spray of Plum Blossoms

费玉清
Fèi Yùqīng
Fei Yu-ching


真情像草原廣闊
Zhēnqíng xiàng cǎoyuán guǎngkuò
True love like the grassland so wide

層層風雨不能阻隔
céng céng fēngyǔ bùnéng zǔgé
Unaffected by layers of wind and rain

總有雲開 日出時候
zǒng yǒu yún kāi rì chū shíhòu
There will be a time when the clouds open and sun shines

萬丈陽光照耀你我
wànzhàng yángguāng zhàoyào nǐ wǒ
A thousand sunbeams fall onto us


真情像梅花開過
zhēnqíng xiàng méihuā kāiguò
True love like plum blossoms blooming

冷冷冰雪不能掩沒
lěng lěng bīngxuě bùnéng yǎn mò 
Unhindered by the cold icy snow

就在最冷 枝頭綻放
jiù zài zuì lěng zhī tóu zhànfàng
At the coldest hour, its branches burst into bloom

看見春天走向你我
kànjiàn chūntiān zǒuxiàng nǐ wǒ
Can see spring nears you and me


雪花飄飄北風蕭蕭
Xuěhuā piāo piāo běifēng xiāoxiāo
Snow petals drift, the north wind whistles

天地  一片 蒼茫
tiāndì yīpiàn cāngmáng
The world ever boundless

一剪寒梅 傲立雪中
yī jiǎn hánméi ào lì xuě zhōng
A spray of winter plum blossoms stands proudly in the snow

只為 伊人 飄香
zhǐ wèi yīrén piāoxiāng
Only for her does its fragrance drift

愛我所愛 無怨無悔
ài wǒ suǒ ài wú yuàn wú huǐ
My love is without complaints and regrets

此情 長留 心間
cǐ qíng zhǎng liú xīn jiān  
This feeling forever present in my heart
