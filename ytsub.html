Subtitled Youtube Video

The idea is to add synced subtitles while a video is playing.

The current motivation is to be able to see the translated lyrics as they play for a recording of Bach's Saint Matthew Passion.

Here's a decent video: Matthäus-Passion (BWV 244) concert by Tölzer Knabenchor & Hofkapelle München, conducted by Christian Fliegner
